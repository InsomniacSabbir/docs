### **open**
<br/>
<hr/>

`open` opens a link in the same browser window.

**Arguments**

-   `URL` ([String](https://developer.mozilla.org/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)) link to open

**Example**

```javascript
1 open('https://workerb.io/')
```
